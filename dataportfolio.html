Business Request & User Stories


The business request for this data analyst project was to create an executive sales report for sales managers. Based on the business's need, the following user stories were designed to ensure delivery and compliance with acceptance criteria throughout the project.


Role Request / Demand User Value Acceptance Criteria
Sales Manager To get a dashboard overview of internet sales Can follow better which customers and products sell the best A Power BI dashboard that updates data once a day
Sales Representative A Detailed Overview of Internet Sales per Customers Can follow up with my customers who buy the most and who we can sell more to A Power BI dashboard that allows me to filter data for each customer
Sales Representative A detailed overview of Internet Sales per Products Can follow up my Products that sell the most A Power BI dashboard that allows me to filter data for each Product
Sales Manager A dashboard overview of Internet sales Follow sales over time against the budget A Power BI dashboard with graphs and KPIs comparing against the budget.
